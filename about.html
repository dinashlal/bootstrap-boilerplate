Bold ideas to make real meaninfgul change in our Community!

Geter-Dune is running on a platform of creative problem-solving and trying new and innovated ways to tackle the challenges that has stalled progress in the Rosedale District. Geter-Dune studied public policy at the Maxwell School of Citizenship and Public Affairs at Syracuse University, graduating with a PHD in Public Administration. In addition, she has earned a MBA from the Whitman School of Business at Syracuse as well. She is academically prepared to bring bold new initiatives to jump start the Rosadale economy, creating opportunities and access in reach of all members of the Rosedale community.
